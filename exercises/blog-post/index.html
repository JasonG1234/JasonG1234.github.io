Fallout 4

By J.Gallagher Feb-12-2026

Story

Fallout 4 follows the "Sole Survivor" who emerges from cryosleep in Vault 111 in 2287, 210 years after a nuclear war, to find their spouse murdered and son, Shaun, kidnapped. Set in the Commonwealth (Boston area), the player navigates, rebuilds settlements, and uncovers a conspiracy involving the Institute. The journey culminates in a choice between four main factions—the Institute, Brotherhood of Steel, Railroad, and Minutemen—to decide the wasteland's future.

Deathclaw

In Fallout 4, Deathclaws are top-tier, aggressive apex predators with thick hides, high health (up to 91+ level variants), and devastating, fast-striking claw attacks. Defeating them requires targeting their weaker belly, crippling their legs to slow them, or using high-damage weaponry like rifles and miniguns. They are found across the Commonwealth and can yield top-tier loot.

Fallouts Factions

The four main factions in Fallout 4, vying for control of the Commonwealth, are the Minutemen, focused on civilian defense; the Brotherhood of Steel, a technologically advanced military force; the Railroad, dedicated to freeing Synths; and the Institute, a shadowy scientific organization creating Synths. Your choices with these groups determine the game's ending.
