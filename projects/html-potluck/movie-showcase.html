The Hunger Games

  GENRE: Science fiction /Action /Drama   RUNTIME:2h 26m
  RATING:


SYNOPSIS

The Hunger Games is a popular dystopian young adult franchise by Suzanne Collins, featuring a televised fight-to-the-death, set in the nation of Panem, where Katniss Everdeen (Jennifer Lawrence) challenges a brutal Capitol. Directed by Gary Ross (first film) and others, the series is lauded for its intense action, social commentary, and themes of survival, rebellion, and love.



Pacific Rim

  GENRE: Action /Science fiction /Monster /Fantasy /Adventure   RUNTIME: 2h 11m
  RATING:


SYNOPSIS

Long ago, legions of monstrous creatures called Kaiju arose from the sea, bringing with them all-consuming war. To fight the Kaiju, mankind developed giant robots called Jaegers, designed to be piloted by two humans locked together in a neural bridge. However, even the Jaegers are not enough to defeat the Kaiju, and humanity is on the verge of defeat. Mankind's last hope now lies with a washed-up ex-pilot, an untested trainee and an old, obsolete Jaeger.



Iron Man

  GENRE: Action /Superhero /War /Science fiction /Adventure /Drama  /Thriller /crime fiction   RUNTIME:2h 6m
  RATING:


SYNOPSIS

Iron Man (2008) follows billionaire genius Tony Stark, who is kidnapped in Afghanistan and forced to build a weapon. Instead, he creates a powerful suit of armor to escape and, upon returning home, vows to use his technology to fight evil and stop his company's weapons from falling into the wrong hands.

  RATING:


SYNOPSIS

When a bank teller discovers he's actually a background player in an open-world video game, he decides to become the hero of his own story -- one that he can rewrite himself.
